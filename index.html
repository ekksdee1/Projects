How to Make Mac n' Cheese

Mac n' Cheese is a classic American dish that consists of boiled noodles and some sort of cheese sauce. It was often used as a struggle food, but now it has become more of a soul/comfort food that people eat. It is a simple dish to make, so here's my recipe for this delicious dish!

Source: Hayden Walker. Overhead Shot of a Plate of Mac and Cheese. 2017. Pexels.com (https://www.pexels.com/photo/overhead-shot-of-a-plate-of-mac-and-cheese-9397238/)

Ingredients

  • Pasta
    • Elbows
    • Rotini
    • Riccoli
    • etc.
  • Cheese Sauce (use any combination of these)
    • Cheddar
    • American
    • Colby Jack
    • Gouda
    • Mozzarella
    • etc.
  • Boiling Water
  • Milk Products
    • Sour Cream
    • Butter
    • Milk


Directions

  1. Boil water
  2. Put the noodles in the boiling water.
  3. Once the noodles are cooked, take them out and set them aside.
  4. Make the cheese sauce:
    1. Melt the cheese(s)
    2. Stir until blended
    3. Add the milk products to the cheese sauce.
  5. Pour the cheese sauce over the cooked noodles.
  6. Enjoy!
